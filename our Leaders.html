Vice Mayor

Simplicia “Bebie” O. Neri


Vice Mayor Simplicia "Bebie" NERI of Ozamiz City attended the 29th National Convention of the Vice Mayors’ League of the Philippines (VMLP) held on November 25–27, 2025, at the Manila Hotel in Manila City. The event gathered Vice Mayors from all over the country to discuss important issues on local governance and to elect the new VMLP National Officers as required by the League’s Constitution, By-Laws, and Election Guidelines.

Congressman

Sancho Fernando “Ando” F. Oaminal


Sancho Fernando "Ando" Fuentes Oaminal is a Filipino politician currently serving as the Representative for the 2nd District of Misamis Occidental. Born on March 19, 1993, in Ozamiz City, he is the son of Governor Henry S. Oaminal Sr. and Pearl Grace Fuentes. His brother, Atty. Henry F. Oaminal Jr., serves as the Mayor of Ozamiz City. Sancho's educational background includes primary education at Ozamiz Montessori Center and secondary education at Ateneo de Manila University, where he also pursued his undergraduate studies. Before his congressional role, he was the Mayor of Ozamiz City from 2019 to 2022 and served as the President of the Misamis Occidental Provincial Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) Federation.
