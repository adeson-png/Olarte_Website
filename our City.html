Welcome to Our City

officially the City of Ozamiz, is a 2nd class component city in the province of Misamis Occidental, Philippines, and is known as the "Gateway to Northwestern Mindanao" and the "Gem of the Panguil Bay". It serves as a key center for commerce, health, transportation, and education in the region.

About Ozamiz City And Geography

Ozamiz City, located in Misamis Occidental, is known for its friendly people, rich heritage, and rapidly growing economy. With its unique blend of history and modern development, the city serves as a gateway to Northwestern Mindanao. Ozamiz is located on the southeast coast of Misamis Occidental. It is nearby the Zamboanga del Sur and Zamboanga del Norte provinces to the west, and Lanao del Norte is across the Panguil Bay. It is bordered by Tangub to the south and Clarin to the north. Ozamiz is 52 kilometers (32 mi) from Maria Cristina Falls, the main source of hydroelectric power in Mindanao.
